Overall Statistics

  • Over 40% of the visa applicants work in IT.
  • Combining Fincance, almost 20% of the visa applicants work in Economic Sector.
  • Other highly technical jobs such as Health Care and Aerospace are also common.
  • H-1B is the most common work visa.
  • After H-1B and L-1 visa, the next most common visa type is student visa (F-1)
  • Overall, the visa acceptance rate is around 80%.
  • The acceptance rate is the highest to the lowest in the order of L-1, H-1B, F-1.
  • Educational and Health Care workers have the 100% acceptance rate for L-1. It is probably due to the fact that there are many international exhanges in the industry.
